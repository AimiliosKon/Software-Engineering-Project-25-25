# Software-Engineering-Project-25-26
Software engineering project of the CEID undergraduate course "Software Technology"

Bin...Go! | Smart Recycling Ecosystem

Bin...Go! is a comprehensive software solution designed to modernize urban waste management and promote environmental consciousness in the city of Patras. By bridging the gap between citizens and municipal services, the platform transforms recycling from a chore into a rewarding, data-driven community experience.
The BinGo platform offers a sophisticated, dual-interface ecosystem designed to streamline urban waste management and foster environmental engagement. For the citizen, the mobile application provides a comprehensive "Green Profile" that tracks recycling history and incentivizes sustainable behavior through gamified rewards, such as points for completing environmental challenges that are redeemable for municipal services or local discounts. Users can access an interactive eco-map featuring real-time visualization of recycling bins categorized by material, complemented by an intelligent eco-routing system that calculates the most efficient navigation paths to avoid overflowing units. Furthermore, an integrated AI Recycling Assistant facilitates the identification of complex materials via camera scans, offering precise preparation instructions to ensure proper disposal. Parallel to these user-centric features, the management dashboard empowers municipal employees with robust network supervision tools, utilizing IoT sensor data and citizen reports to monitor bin capacity in real-time. This administrative suite further enhances operational efficiency through automated collection optimization based on fill-rate statistics and a dedicated maintenance tracking system for the digital reporting of damaged infrastructure
